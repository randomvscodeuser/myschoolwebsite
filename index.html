Our logo and crest

  • The logo

    Our school logo in the shape of a shield symbolises our strength and our commitment in nurturing the whole child. The logo consists of a beacon with five brilliant rays in golden yellow serving as the guiding light. The rays signify our aspiration for every Beacon to be a Contributor, Connector, Constructor, Conductor and Confident learner. Golden yellow, a colour for joy and creativity, depicts the school as a happy place to learn and work in and where innovation is part of the school culture. Royal blue represents space, openness and an environment that is engaging and conducive for learning. The globe in the background highlights vastness and possibilities in a global and connected world in which our learners expand their horizons and envision the future. The design also portrays the journey of a pathfinder charting new directions and striving to be a peak of excellence. It reinforces our aspiration for each Beacon to be a peak of excellence in his or her chosen areas. The school motto, "SHINE", at the base of the crest indicates that our pursuits are guided by our core values.
